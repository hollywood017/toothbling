Frequently Asked Questions

Q. Does the application procedure hurt?

A. Not at all. The procedure is similar to placing an orthodontic bracket (braces) on a tooth. There is no drilling involved and the procedure is completely painless.

Q. Will I feel the crystal on my tooth?

A. Tooth Bling's Swarovski Crystals are small in diameter. Initially something on your tooth will feel different, but only for a few days after the application.

Q. How long will tooth jewelry last?

A. The longevity of a Tooth Bling Jewel on the tooth depends entirely on the individual client. The natural acidity of your saliva and diet plays a big part on how long an application lasts. The average time however, would be approximately 6 months to a couple years.

*Restrictions to diet will result in longer retention time.
(avoid gum, sticky candy, soda)

Q. Will the jewel harm my tooth?

A. No! The jewel is bonded to the enamel like a orthodontic bracket without any drilling or pain. The procedure and removal can be compared to the placement and removal of orthodontic brackets. They can be removed or replaced anytime has no effect on the tooth enamel once the jewel is removed or replaced.

Q. Can I have a jewel placed on any tooth surface?

A. No. The jewel can only be placed on natural, clean, healthy enamel. It can not bond to crowns, veneers or dentures. We recommend placing it on top half of upper teeth or lower half of bottom teeth. Otherwise, it may come off due to natural chewing forces.

Q. What if the jewel falls off and I swallow it?

A. The jewels are tiny glass crystals or gold. They have no sharp edges. It will basically come out "The natural way".

Q. Can I still have my teeth cleaned by my dentist/hygienist?

A. Yes, you can and should still have you teeth cleaned by a professional. Tooth Bling jewelry will not cause any problems for a hygienist to clean your teeth. The area underneath the jewel is sealed like a filling and will not let in any bacteria. The hygenist can easily polish on top and around the jewel with a soft point.
