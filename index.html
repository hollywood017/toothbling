Add Sparkle and Elegance to your Smile...

The latest craze isn't just teeth whitening anymore, but a fashion statement that has added a new meaning to the term a "gorgeous smile!"

Tooth Bling's Swarovski Tooth Jewels are simple, subtle and shimmering! Swarovski is considered to produce the finest precision-cut crystals in th world. Their crystals are polished to the highest standards of quality and sparkle like diamonds.

Our tooth jewelry is made by Genuine Swarovski Crystals that are small in diameter, have a flat back platinum surface and are applied to the tooth using safe dental materials. Our application requires no drilling and no holes. It is completely painless, will not harm the tooth, and can be easily removed. It is perfect for those who want a bit of glamour and fun!

Our gold jewelry Yellow & White Gold Tooth Jewels are 22 or 18 carate Gold jewels. They're all approx. 3mm in size. Some of our designs have 0.02 ct diamond center.
